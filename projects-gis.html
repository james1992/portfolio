UW Bike Racks Link

A public map that my department produces to allow campus visitors to locate public bike racks on the Seattle Campus of the University of Washington.
UW Buildings Link

A public map that includes all buildings currently owned or leased by the University of Washington. The map allows visitors to dynamically query the data.
UW Campus Memorials Link

A map of all memorials on the Seattle Campus of the University of Washington, these memorials include both trees and benches.
UW Trees Link

This map includes the location of all trees on the Seattle Campus of the University of Washington. As in the other maps, clicking on a tree pulls up a popup windows with information about the tree.
